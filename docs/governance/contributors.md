# AIOGF Contributors

This page recognizes individuals who have contributed to the development, review, or improvement of the AI Operational Governance Framework (AIOGF).  
Contributions may include content drafting, technical review, structural improvements, documentation enhancements, or other meaningful support.

Contributors are listed voluntarily and may request updates to their entry at any time.

---

## How Contributors Are Recognized

Contributors may be acknowledged for:

- **Content Contributions** — drafting or improving supplements, annexes, or controls  
- **Technical Review** — identifying issues, improving clarity, or validating structure  
- **Documentation Support** — improving templates, diagrams, or metadata  
- **Framework Enhancements** — proposing new controls, roadmap items, or structural improvements  

Recognition does **not** imply ownership of contributed material.  
All contributions remain governed by the *Contributor Terms of Use* and the *AI‑OGF Limited Use License*.
