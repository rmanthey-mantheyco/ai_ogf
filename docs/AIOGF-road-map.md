# AI-OGF Road‑Map / Backlog

This roadmap outlines planned, deferred, and future enhancements for the AI Operational Governance Framework (AI-OGF). These items are intentionally excluded from the current release but represent high‑value areas for expansion as the framework matures.

The roadmap is divided into three categories:
- **Framework Content Enhancements**
- **Documentation & Structural Improvements**
- **Site & Publishing Enhancements**

---

## Framework Content Road‑Map

### **9.x — AI Kill‑Switch & Emergency Controls**
Future expansion of the 9.x family will focus on emergency response, containment, and rapid‑shutdown mechanisms for AI systems.

Planned items:
- **9.3.x Emergency Containment Controls** *(new family)*
- **9.3.1 Emergency Containment Controls** *(first supplement in this family)*

---

### **10.x — AI Isolation & Air‑Gaps (Future Enhancements)**
Additional supplements may expand the operational isolation and testing capabilities of AI systems.

Potential additions:
- **10.3.x Operational Isolation Controls**
- **10.4.x Isolation Testing Procedures**

---

### **11.x — Environmental Isolation & Drift Controls**
Future enhancements may extend environmental monitoring and predictive capabilities.

Potential additions:
- **11.3.x Environmental Drift Prediction**
- **11.4.x Safety Instrumentation**

---

### **12.x — Environmental Hazard Controls**
This family may expand to include hazard modeling and scenario‑based safety controls.

Potential additions:
- **12.3.x Hazard Simulation Controls**
- **12.4.x Hazard‑Aware AI Behavior Models**

---

## Documentation & Structural Enhancements

Planned improvements to documentation clarity, navigation, and contributor support:

- Family overview pages for **4.x–12.x**
- Formal Road‑Map page (this document)
- Contributor Guide *(future addition)*
- Versioning page for tracking releases (e.g., v1.3 → future versions)

---

## Site & Publishing Enhancements

Optional enhancements to improve the published documentation experience:

- Branding elements (logo, footer, color scheme)
- Collapsible sidebar navigation
- Automated PDF export workflow
- “Last updated” metadata for each page

---

## Road‑Map Philosophy

AI-OGF is designed to evolve as AI systems, operational risks, and industry practices change.  
This roadmap will be updated as new requirements emerge, new contributors participate, and the framework expands into deeper technical and operational domains.
